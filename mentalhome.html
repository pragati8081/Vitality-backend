Frequently Asked Questions

What are the charges? ▼
Our standard charges are: Counselling Psychologist - INR2300 Clinical Psychologist - INR2700 For further details, visit our Pricing.page.
How do I schedule a session? ▼
You can book a session by messaging us on WhatsApp (+919205025183) Instagram (@thefriendlycouch) For further details, visit our Therapy page.
Can you help me get diagnosed? ▼
Yes, the clinical psychologists on our team offer diagnosis/diagnostic evaluation. Please visit our Diagnosis Page for more information.
How many sessions will I need? ▼
The therapist will recommend an initial session to assess your concerns and discuss a suitable frequency for ongoing sessions. There is no strict schedule you need to adhere to; you are free to choose a session frequency that aligns with your comfort and preferences.
Are trial/introductory sessions available? ▼
We do not provide introductory calls or trial/free sessions to ensure fair compensation for the therapists' time, effort, and expertise in assisting individuals. However, you are welcome to engage in a brief conversation via WhatsApp chat/messages before scheduling your first session. Feel free to explore this opportunity without any pressure or obligation to continue with therapy. The decision to proceed is entirely yours.
Do you provide in-person therapy? ▼
We currently only offer online therapy over audio & video calls.
